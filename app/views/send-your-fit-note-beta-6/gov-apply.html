6. How to send your fit note

After you’ve made a claim for ESA you’ll need to send your fit note to the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP).

You might also know a fit note as sick note, doctor's note, medical certificate or statement of fitness for work.

Online

Send your fit note online using a phone, tablet, desktop computer or laptop.

This is the quickest way to get your fit note to DWP.
